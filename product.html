Paushtika

The smart collar integrates advanced sensors to monitor key physiological and behavioral parameters such as body temperature, heart rate, movement, rumination activity, and location through GPS. These sensors continuously collect data and transmit it to a cloud platform via wireless technologies like LoRa, GSM, or BLE. Machine learning algorithms analyze the patterns to detect early signs of illness, abnormalities in behavior, heat cycles for breeding, and overall wellness status.

Farmers receive instant alerts on their mobile application, helping them make quick decisions regarding treatment, nutrition, or isolation of sick animals. This system minimizes disease spread, reduces veterinary costs, and significantly prevents losses due to delayed diagnosis. The collar is designed to be lightweight, robust, waterproof, and energy-efficient, making it suitable for rural environments and long-term usage.

By enabling predictive health monitoring and reducing manual dependency, Paushtika enhances farm productivity, improves livestock welfare, and supports data-driven dairy management. This project has the potential to transform traditional cattle rearing into a modern, technology-enabled practice, empowering farmers with reliable, real-time insights to improve their income and overall herd health.

Purpose

To develop a smart cattle healthcare system enabling real-time health monitoring, early disease detection, and improved farmer income.

Features

● Real-time insights even in remote areas

● Herd tracking & performance optimization

● Early disease detection

● Affordable & scalable

Tech Used

● AI-Powered Predictive Analytics

● Hybrid IoT Connectivity

● Edge Computing

● Blockchain Health Records

● Solar-Powered Devices

NutriTrack

NutriTrack is an innovative smart feeding bottle designed to help mothers monitor and manage their baby's nutrition with ease and confidence. Equipped with advanced sensors and a connected mobile app, NutriTrack automatically measures milk quantity, tracks feeding duration, and monitors temperature in real time to ensure every feed is safe, healthy, and perfectly timed.

Its built-in alerts notify you when the milk is reaching unsafe temperatures or when it's time for the next feed, making caregiving effortless. With a hygienic, BPA-free design and smart sterilization reminders, NutriTrack combines modern technology with everyday practicality, giving parents the peace of mind they deserve. Whether at home or on the go, NutriTrack ensures smarter feeding, healthier habits, and happier babies.

Today, NutriTrack stands as a symbol of how ADI and IoT can blend to deliver meaningful, life-enhancing solutions for parents, infants, and caregivers.

Purpose

To develop a smart feeding bottle that monitors baby feeding patterns and provides personalized recommendations to parents.

Features

● Track feeding habits

● Milk consumption estimation

● Temperature monitoring

● Sterilization reminders

● Data sharing with caregivers

Tech Used

● ESP32 Microcontroller

● Temperature & Weight Sensors

● Cloud Integration

● Mobile App
